MDCAT 2025: Your Gateway to Medical Education

The Medical and Dental College Admission Test (MDCAT) is a standardized entrance examination required for admission to medical and dental colleges in Pakistan. The test evaluates candidates' knowledge in key subjects essential for medical education.

MDCAT 2025 is expected to be more competitive than ever, with thousands of students competing for limited seats in medical colleges. Our comprehensive preparation program is designed to give you the competitive edge needed to secure your place in a reputable medical institution.

MDCAT 2025 Syllabus Details

The MDCAT 2025 examination consists of 180 multiple-choice questions distributed across different subjects as follows:

Subject Number of Questions Weightage
Biology 81 MCQs 45%
Chemistry 45 MCQs 25%
Physics 36 MCQs 20%
English 9 MCQs 5%
Logical Reasoning 9 MCQs 5%

Important Note: The MDCAT is a computer-based test with a total duration of 3.5 hours. Negative marking may apply for incorrect answers, so strategic preparation is essential.
